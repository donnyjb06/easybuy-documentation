## What they do

These screen components compose the recovery and verification flows that are currently shipped:

- `RequestPasswordResetForm`
- `VerifyEmailForm`
- `VerifyPasswordResetForm`

## Why they exist

These screens are where the shared component layer, the hook layer, and the auth provider meet. They show how the current product scope actually behaves.

## Usage

These are route-level screen components. They do not expose a wide public prop surface. Instead, they depend on the auth context and their matching hook.
