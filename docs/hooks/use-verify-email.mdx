## What it does

`useVerifyEmail` coordinates OTP submission, resend handling, cooldown state, error handling, and redirect behavior for the verify-email screen.

## Why it exists

This flow has enough moving parts that the screen component would become noisy if it owned all of the behavior inline. The hook isolates that orchestration.

## Used by

- `VerifyEmailForm`
